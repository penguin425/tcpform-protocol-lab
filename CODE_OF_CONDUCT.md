# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to making participation in tcpform a harassment-free and
welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of experience, identity,
background, ability, or personal characteristics.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, patient, and constructive.
- Discuss ideas and code rather than attacking people.
- Give actionable feedback and assume good intent while still addressing harm.
- Respect privacy, confidentiality, and responsible disclosure boundaries.
- Help newcomers learn the project and its development process.
- Accept maintainer decisions gracefully after concerns have been heard.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, discrimination, or sexualized attention.
- Insults, personal attacks, trolling, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing another person's private information without permission.
- Sharing credentials, private captures, embargoed vulnerabilities, or other
  confidential material.
- Any conduct that would reasonably make participation unsafe or unwelcome.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to `penguin@valuemix.jp`. Reports will be
reviewed promptly and handled as confidentially as practical. Maintainers may
edit or remove content, issue a warning, temporarily restrict participation, or
permanently ban a participant based on the severity and pattern of behavior.

Retaliation against someone who reports a concern is prohibited. A maintainer
who is directly involved in a report must not make the final enforcement
decision alone.

## Scope

This policy applies in repository spaces and when someone is representing the
project in public or private community interactions.
